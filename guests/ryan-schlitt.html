Guest Speaker

Ryan Schlitt

Managing Director and Global Head, Private Capital Advisory Group, Piper Sandler

Ryan Schlitt is a Managing Director and Global Head of the Private Capital Advisory Group at Piper Sandler, where he has helped build one of the most comprehensive private capital advisory practices in the market. Having been present at the formation of major secondary platforms including Coller Capital and Strategic Partners (now Blackstone), Schlitt argues the secondary market is still in its early innings given the sheer volume of private assets that will require future liquidity. He forecasts alternatives AUM growing from $6.6 trillion to over $11 trillion by 2029 at a 13.8% CAGR.

Topics covered on Liquid Courage:
  • Piper Sandler identification of 30,000 PE-owned businesses held over five years in the US
  • Pure math argument for continued secondary market growth as private assets multiply
  • Continuation vehicles as a secular trend, not just a temporal liquidity response
  • Growth of retail and private wealth alternatives access and its secondary implications
  • Early career lessons from fundraising for Coller Capital and Strategic Partners
  • Active second half 2025 and 2026 outlook for traditional secondary liquidity
  • 13.8% CAGR forecast for alternatives AUM through 2029
  • Three fund situation types driving secondary flow: wind-ups, struggling exits, and trophy assets

Welcome to Liquid Courage!

About Liquid Courage

Liquid Courage is a video-podcast presenting thought leadership and research on the global private capital secondaries markets. On a regular basis, Liquid Courage convenes expert conversations about the investment opportunities and macro themes driving the secondaries market, including GP-led transactions, continuation vehicles, LP portfolio management, and the evolving role of secondaries across private equity and private credit.

Liquid Courage is hosted by financial journalist David Snow, a long-time chronicler of the alternative investment market.
