Guest Speaker

Ryan Binette

Managing Director and Co-Head of Secondary Capital Advisory, Piper Sandler

Ryan Binette is a Managing Director and Co-Head of Secondary Capital Advisory at Piper Sandler, advising institutional investors on secondary portfolio transactions. He helps LPs understand that the opportunity cost of holding underperforming fund interests often outweighs the pain of accepting a discount, and that a well-structured competitive process can demonstrate genuine market-clearing value. Binette sees growing LP sophistication in using the secondary market proactively for portfolio rebalancing rather than as a last resort.

Topics covered on Liquid Courage:
  • Opportunity cost analysis of holding versus selling tail-end fund interests
  • Lp portfolio rebalancing using secondary market transactions
  • Competitive sale process design to maximize secondary transaction value
  • Challenges of attracting buyers for deeply discounted tail-end positions
  • Early secondary sales driven by LP sector and strategy repositioning
  • Gp-lp relationship dynamics in LP-initiated secondary transactions
  • Buyer universe segmentation across secondary portfolio processes
  • Private equity fund performance peak at year nine and its implications

Welcome to Liquid Courage!

About Liquid Courage

Liquid Courage is a video-podcast presenting thought leadership and research on the global private capital secondaries markets. On a regular basis, Liquid Courage convenes expert conversations about the investment opportunities and macro themes driving the secondaries market, including GP-led transactions, continuation vehicles, LP portfolio management, and the evolving role of secondaries across private equity and private credit.

Liquid Courage is hosted by financial journalist David Snow, a long-time chronicler of the alternative investment market.
