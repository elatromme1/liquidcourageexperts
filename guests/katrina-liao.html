Guest Speaker

Katrina Liao

Partner, Investment, Coller Capital

Katrina Liao is a Partner at Coller Capital, one of the world's leading dedicated secondary investment managers. She brings both the buyer and LP perspectives to discussions of continuation vehicles, noting that the embedded knowledge an existing LP has about the underlying company makes a CV roll decision structurally superior to most co-investment opportunities. Liao shares insight from the Coller Barometer survey findings on LP attitudes toward CVs and the market's continued maturation.

Topics covered on Liquid Courage:
  • Buyer perspective on continuation vehicle quality and GP alignment
  • Coller Barometer findings on LP roll versus cash-out behavior in CVs
  • How CV due diligence differs from traditional fund underwriting
  • Gp-led versus LP-led market split and its evolution over two years
  • Competition among secondary buyers affecting pricing versus economics
  • Evergreen and perpetual capital fund strategies in secondary investing
  • Gp economics rollover as the key signal of CV quality
  • Cvs squared and the maturing of the GP-led secondary market

Welcome to Liquid Courage!

About Liquid Courage

Liquid Courage is a video-podcast presenting thought leadership and research on the global private capital secondaries markets. On a regular basis, Liquid Courage convenes expert conversations about the investment opportunities and macro themes driving the secondaries market, including GP-led transactions, continuation vehicles, LP portfolio management, and the evolving role of secondaries across private equity and private credit.

Liquid Courage is hosted by financial journalist David Snow, a long-time chronicler of the alternative investment market.
