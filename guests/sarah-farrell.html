Guest Speaker

Sarah Farrell

Managing Principal and Head of Private Equity Europe and Asia, Allstate

Sarah Farrell is a Managing Principal and Head of Private Equity for Europe and Asia at Allstate, one of America's largest insurance companies. As a sophisticated LP, she offers candid insights into how institutional investors navigate continuation vehicles—from governance concerns and valuation conflicts to the practical decision of whether to roll or take liquidity. Farrell serves on the ILPA board and is a leading voice for LP-friendly CV structuring standards in the global private capital market.

Topics covered on Liquid Courage:
  • Lp decision-making framework for continuation vehicle roll or sell decisions
  • Conflict-of-interest governance in GP-led secondary transactions
  • Valuation and fairness opinion limitations in continuation vehicle processes
  • Importance of independent third-party pricing and LPAC consultation
  • Ilpa guidance and LP protections in continuation vehicle structures
  • Deal-killer terms that make a CV unattractive for institutional LPs
  • Cvs squared and what they signal about private market maturity
  • Evergreen and perpetual capital vehicles and their secondary market role

Welcome to Liquid Courage!

About Liquid Courage

Liquid Courage is a video-podcast presenting thought leadership and research on the global private capital secondaries markets. On a regular basis, Liquid Courage convenes expert conversations about the investment opportunities and macro themes driving the secondaries market, including GP-led transactions, continuation vehicles, LP portfolio management, and the evolving role of secondaries across private equity and private credit.

Liquid Courage is hosted by financial journalist David Snow, a long-time chronicler of the alternative investment market.
