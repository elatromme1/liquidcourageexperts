Guest Speaker

Matthew Ahern

Managing Principal, Knightsbridge Advisors

Matthew Ahern is the Managing Principal of Knightsbridge Advisors, a secondary investment specialist focused on venture capital. He argues that with roughly $8 trillion in unrealized venture capital value globally, secondary transactions have become the second-most popular source of liquidity in the VC ecosystem. Ahern brings expertise in evaluating manager credibility, the dynamics of companies staying private longer, and the role of AI adoption in determining which venture funds will generate lasting value.

Topics covered on Liquid Courage:
  • Scale of the venture capital liquidity problem and $8 trillion in unrealized value
  • Secondary market becoming the second-most popular VC liquidity channel
  • Valuation credibility differences between established and emerging VC managers
  • Impact of AI adoption on existing software company valuations in VC portfolios
  • Fund-level versus direct company secondaries in the venture capital market
  • Multi-asset continuation vehicles as an emerging tool for venture GPs
  • Winner-takes-all return dynamics and the importance of manager selection
  • Technological obsolescence risk in tail-end venture fund portfolios

About Liquid Courage

Liquid Courage is a video-podcast presenting thought leadership and research on the global private capital secondaries markets. On a regular basis, Liquid Courage convenes expert conversations about the investment opportunities and macro themes driving the secondaries market, including GP-led transactions, continuation vehicles, LP portfolio management, and the evolving role of secondaries across private equity and private credit.

Liquid Courage is hosted by financial journalist David Snow, a long-time chronicler of the alternative investment market.
